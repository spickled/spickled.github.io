Abbi Skota

Position: Head Scientist

Time With Us: indefinite

Date of death: to be determined

History

Abbi officially joined the company when she was 15 years old. She was incredibly efficiant with the equipment since her mother worked for the company for many years. Her father worked from home, but did similar work. Many people claim that she was sweet and friendly during their collaborations. Over time they started to see a shift in her behavior, becoming more aggressive and irritated by the simplist of things. Her eventual promotion to head scientist came earlier than everyone expected, but upon being told the good news she kept a blank face. The next day she was more pale than normal and anyone that held an object after her was shocked to find it so cold. Almost like something drained all of her passion.

Everyone was worried about her, but nothing was brought up during the boost in business. They were extremely busy due to some new results that required everyone's attention. Even people that didn't have any experience in the field.

Abbi's relationship with Petrona is unknown to anyone since Petrona dissapeared. Not a single tear dropped from her eye, makes sense since she knew what exactly happened to her.
